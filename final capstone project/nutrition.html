🍽️ The Healthy Plate Rule
🥦
½ Plate – Vegetables & FruitsFill half your plate with colourful vegetables and 1–2 servings of fruit.
🌾
¼ Plate – Whole GrainsChoose brown rice, whole wheat, oats or millets over refined carbohydrates.
🥩
¼ Plate – Lean ProteinInclude dal, eggs, fish, chicken, tofu, or legumes for protein needs.
💧
Drink WaterMake water your primary beverage. Aim for 8–10 glasses (2–3 L) daily.
