📊 BMI Categories Explained
Below 18.5
Underweight
May indicate nutritional deficiency or other health issues. Consult a doctor.
18.5 – 24.9
Normal
Healthy weight range. Maintain your lifestyle with balanced diet and exercise.
25.0 – 29.9
Overweight
Slight risk of heart disease and diabetes. Improve diet and increase activity.
30.0 and above
Obese
High risk of serious conditions. Seek medical guidance for a healthy plan.
⚠️ Disclaimer: BMI is a general screening tool and does not account for muscle mass, bone density, or age. Always consult a healthcare professional for a complete health assessment.
