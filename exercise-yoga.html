Yoga

An ancient mind-body practice combining postures, breathing, and meditation. It builds flexibility, strength, and mental calm — one of the most holistic forms of exercise for all ages.

✅ Benefits

  • Improves flexibility, balance, and joint mobility
  • Builds core and muscular strength gradually
  • Reduces stress, anxiety, and cortisol levels significantly
  • Improves posture and spinal health
  • Supports heart health and lowers blood pressure
  • Enhances sleep quality and mental focus
  • Helpful in managing diabetes and thyroid conditions

⏱️ Recommended Duration

  • Beginners: 20–30 minutes, 3 times per week
  • Intermediate: 45–60 minutes, 4–5 times per week
  • Morning practice is ideal for mental clarity and energy
  • Even 10 minutes of pranayama (breathing) daily is beneficial
  • Consistency matters far more than duration

👥 Who Can Do It

  • Suitable for all ages including seniors and children
  • Excellent for people with back pain or poor posture
  • Safe for managing stress, anxiety, or depression
  • Gentle yoga is appropriate during pregnancy (ask doctor)
  • People recovering from injury with medical clearance

⚠️ Safety Tips

  • Start with a certified instructor if you are a beginner
  • Never force a pose — always respect your body's limits
  • Avoid hot yoga if you have heart conditions or are pregnant
  • Use a non-slip mat on a flat, stable surface
  • Practise on an empty stomach (2–3 hours after eating)
  • Inform your instructor of any injuries or health conditions
